// 002 — ABOUT

I show up early, before the crowd finds the good light.

I'm a Michigan-based photographer who spends most weekends in parking lots, alleyways, and showroom floors — wherever the cars are. My work leans documentary: real meets, real light, real crowds pressing in with their phones up.

Whether it's a seven-figure hypercar or a patina'd 911 parked behind a bar, I shoot both the same way — with respect for the machine and patience for the moment it actually looks like itself.
